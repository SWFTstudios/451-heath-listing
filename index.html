About This Home

663 sq ft of Comfortable City Living

Welcome to 451 Heath Place, Apt 2 — a bright, garden-style 1-bedroom condo tucked into a quiet courtyard setting in Hackensack, NJ. Step inside and head up the private staircase to your sun-filled retreat on the second floor.

The open floor plan makes excellent use of 663 square feet — a large, light-filled living and dining area flows naturally through the space. The updated kitchen features new flooring, fresh countertops, and a brand-new refrigerator. The bedroom comfortably fits a king-size bed, and additional furniture is available on request.

Heat, hot water, and parking are all included in rent. Move in knowing your biggest utility costs are covered — rare for Bergen County.
